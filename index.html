HI, I'M Rohit!

I’m a web designer and developer with a great passion for building new things from scratch. I live in the charming city of Lisbon where I create beautiful websites, just like the one you’re about to build in one of my courses. I love everything about tech, riding my bike, and the beach in the summer.

As an online instructor, I get to mix my two great passions: building beautiful websites and teaching. If I had to describe my coding courses in one sentence,These are exactly the courses I wish I had when I first started with web design and development”.

I would say I want to spare you the hassle of going through uncountable books and articles, so I packed everything I learned about building websites right into my video courses.

I guarentee 100% to offer the best value in all my courses, so you can achieve your goals! However, if you don't find value in them for any reason, I will give you a full refund of your money during 30 days. Plus, I will be available to answer all the questions you might have along your coding journey.

So, let's start learning today!
